Phase 1: Experimentation & Learning

Goal: Build foundational knowledge and identify promising use cases through hands-on exploration without pressure.

AI Exploration Guild

Launch a Community of Practice for peer learning and sharing findings.

Sandbox Environments

Provide curated AI tools and dedicated time (10-20%) for experimentation on non-critical tasks.

Foundational Training

Cover LLM basics, prompt engineering, and ethical AI use to build a common understanding.

Focus on Qualitative KPIs

Success in this phase is measured by engagement: the number of experiments run, the diversity of tools explored, and the quality of engineer feedback and identified use cases.
